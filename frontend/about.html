About Smart City Complaint System

Our Mission

We empower citizens and city managers with a unified platform for reporting, tracking, and resolving urban issues.

The system combines complaint submission, AI-based prioritization, live traffic mapping, air quality monitoring, and admin analytics.

Citizen First

Designed for easy reporting and clear status updates.

Smart Insights

AI and data visualization support faster decision making.

Why It Matters

City services work better when citizens can communicate issues directly and officials can respond quickly.

This project promotes transparency, accountability and faster urban maintenance.
